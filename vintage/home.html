When you build your "tomorrow", don't forget about your "now".

To see and to observe is different.
As to live and to exist is different.

If you here, i know you have a little free time, so i would like to ask you to stop and think in peace of what is actually important to you.
The life can be very fast at the time when you're at work, when you're in school and just busy with your daily routine.
But do you tend to think you're in the present during this? Try to be in the present when there is no work, not a thing for your mind to be
busy about, what will you think about? Will it be the now: the wind that you feel if you stepped outside; the soft couch you're laying at;
the smile that's forming on your face? And even now, are you present? Just focus on what you feel right now physically, maybe you are tired
of sitting in this pose, change your position to be comfortable in every way possible, cause there is some time to not do anything but to
be lazy: to focus on the 'NOW'. This page reminds you how important it is to be grateful for the moment you are currently in, of the wind
on your cheeks, the sun on your skin and the shining stars in the skies. Just feel this peace, and you'll be sure: the best is now.
